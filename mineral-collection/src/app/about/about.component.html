About Kunstkamera

Welcome to Kunstkamera – a digital cabinet of curiosities, where science, history, and aesthetics converge. This space is dedicated to the exploration of minerals, astrophotography, and forgotten knowledge, blending the precision of scientific observation with the charm of vintage archives.

What you will find here:

  • Minerals & Meteorites – A personal collection of specimens, cataloged with care and curiosity.
  • Astrophotography – Capturing the silent grandeur of the cosmos through telescopic observation.
  • Thoughts & Notes – A growing repository of scientific musings and forgotten fragments of history.
